Hi there! We're Team RVA.

We are a 2015 fellowship team at Code for America, a non-profit based in San Francisco that seeks to make government services simpler, more effective, and easier to use.

Our team is working with the city of Richmond, VA and community partners to improve health care service delivery for people in poverty.

We build open source technology and organize a network of people dedicated to improving government services. You can contact Ben Golder, Emma Smithayer, or Sam Matthews at richmond@codeforamerica.org.
